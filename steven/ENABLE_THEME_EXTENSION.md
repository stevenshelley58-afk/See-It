## How to Enable "See It" in Your Theme

### Step 1: Go to Theme Editor
1. Open your Shopify Admin
2. Navigate to **Online Store → Themes**
3. Click **Customize** on your active theme

### Step 2: Add the App Block
1. In the theme editor, go to any **Product page** (use the page selector at the top)
2. Look for **"Product information"** section or similar
3. Click **"Add block"**
4. Under **"Apps"** section, you should see **"See It Extension"**
5. Click to add it
6. Position it where you want (usually after Add to Cart button)
7. Click **Save** in the top right

### Alternative Method - App Embeds:
1. In theme editor sidebar, scroll down to find **"App embeds"**
2. You should see **"See It Extension"**
3. Toggle it ON
4. Click **Save**

## What You'll See:
- A **"See it in your room"** button on product pages
- Only shows for products marked as "ready" 
- Clicking opens a modal where customers can:
  - Upload a room photo
  - See the product in their space
  - Adjust size and position

## Troubleshooting:

### If "See It Extension" doesn't appear:
1. Refresh your browser
2. Wait 2-3 minutes for Shopify to sync
3. Try logging out and back into Shopify admin
4. Clear browser cache

### To Test:
1. Visit a product page that shows "ready" status in your app
2. You should see the "See it in your room" button
3. Click it to test the modal
